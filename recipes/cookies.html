Chocolate Chip Cookies

Here is my favorite recipe for chocolate chip cookies. They are soft, chewy, and full of chocolate chips. I hope you enjoy them!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda.
  3. In a large bowl, beat together the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, salt, and vanilla extract until creamy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Gradually beat in the flour mixture.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
  10. Enjoy with a glass of milk!
